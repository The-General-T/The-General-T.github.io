Welcome to the Invoicing System! Here are some tips:

Keep in mind, these buttons will make sense.

  • Add Item: Enter the Item Name, the price, then click "Add Item". This is used for new customers, and any customers we may have "issues" with.
  • Add Tax Exempt: Same steps, but click "Add Tax Exempt". This is used for returning/loyal customers as a way to "keep their service"
  • Clear Transaction: Use this after you download/print your invoice.
  • Complete Transaction: When you/your administrator has finalized your invoice, press "Complete Transaction". Then, click Print or Download.
